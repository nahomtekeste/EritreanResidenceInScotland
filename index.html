Our Objectives (ዕላማታትና)

    ERS wants Eritrean seekers and refugees to be:
  • Supported and relieved from hardship and distress by local people.
  • Empowered to rebuild their lives, to fulfil their potential and to take control of their lives.
  • Integrated settled and safe. ERS wants Scotland to be a place of welcome and understanding;
    and for the general public, organisations, politicians and policy makers to understand the needs of us.

    • Note : ERS (Eritrean Residence In Scotland)..

Eritrean In Scotland.

The Eritrean Organization Center in Scotland is an initiative designed and run independently by a group of Eritrean refugee seeker. Established in November 2019, the center aims to provide Eritrean people with a safe space as well as access to important services. This center is the first and only one of its kind,utilizing a grass roots approach to support and em Eritrean people in a fundamental way. Representing a safe space, the Center stands as a community fixture recognized as the location people can turn to in order to access information, services and support.
